CHAT Virginity Club is run by a community of Virgin Enthusiasts who currently attendTanenbaum CHAT. We are not affiliated with Tanenbaum CHAT.

The executive members behind CHAT Virginity Club are on a mission to make everyone feel accepted and welcome at Tanenbaum CHAT, no matter their physical virginity status.
